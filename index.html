Our Story

Born from a
love of wild places

SerendiBity Vacations & Travel Ideas is a small local business startup, initiated by a young Sri Lankan who loves wildlife and adventure. Our vision: to develop diverse, unique and quality tourism that makes Sri Lanka a unique destination, globally.

Today, 8% of company revenue goes directly to the SerendiBity conservation fund — supporting wildlife rescue, habitat enrichment, and conservation education in rural communities.
